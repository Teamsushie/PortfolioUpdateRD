2018 Highlight Reel

2018 was a tough one for me. Early in May, our car was broken into while on a trip to San Francisco. Everything of mine was stolen. All of my camera equipment, my backup hard drives, even my toothbrush - was gone. My girlfriend lost her passport and laptop. Things were looking bleak. We never recovered any of the stolen items. Fortunately for us, Insurance covered about a third of the stolen items, and in October I was able to pick up camera equipment once again. The following footage was shot from Oct - Dec 31st.
